Group Discussion
After completing all three rounds, discuss and record:
Q1 · Understand
Why does the cell need three separate checkpoints instead of one big check at the end? What specific problem does each one catch?
Q2 · Apply
A drug blocks all cells in metaphase. Based on Round 3, which checkpoint does this drug target — and which protein is it likely affecting?
Q3 · Bridge to Lesson 2
If p53 stopped working, trace what would happen step by step. At which checkpoint does the first problem occur? What does the cell do that it shouldn't?
